Quick game description:
In Lucky Son of a Gun, the player’s main reason for interacting is to land successful shots against enemies whilst avoiding damage. This allows the player to advance to the next interacion

Player Interaction:
The player aims and fires in real time to defeat an enemy using the mouse. This will also visibly roll a player selected dice. 
The player can move around the game world in real time using the keys to avoid bullets.
Players can use the mouse right click to slow down the real time for a focus shot which increases your chance to hit.
