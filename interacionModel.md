(5 Points) Written description of the interaction model, including the objective of the interaction

Description:
In Lucky Son of a Gun, the player’s main reason for interacting is to land successful shots against enemies whilst avoiding damage. This allows the player to advance to the next interacion

Player Interaction w/ Objectives:
The player aims and fires in real time to defeat an enemy using the mouse. This will also visibly roll a player selected dice. 
The player can move around the game world in real time using the keys to avoid bullets.
Players can use the mouse right click to slow down the real time for a focus shot which increases your chance to hit. 

---

(5 Points) Brief write-up on how this interaction model can be used in different parts of the game and why

Player movement interaction is used in all parts of the game and affects the player survivability. A player would not survive for long in this game as avoiding bullets is a main mechanic. 

Player shooting w/ mouse interaction is another critical mechanic that deals damage to enemies attempting to hurt the player. Shooting moves the player closer to their goal of completing a level/clearing a round. This is also the main way that a player will interact with the dice roll mechanic that makes my game slightly different than a standard shoot em up.

Finally, the player interacting with the game to slow down time for better shot aim is a mechanic that's meant to lower the skill ceiling and allow for the player to skip parts of the luck element for a limited time. It'll also include more strategy into the game as the player has to pick and choose when to use the limited time slow down power up and find/create opportunities where they can increase that limited amount.
