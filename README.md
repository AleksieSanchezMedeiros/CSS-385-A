# Elevator Pitch

Lucky Son of a Gun is a fast-paced top-down shooter where every power-up is a gamble. You play as a lone gunslinger in a chaotic world where every pickup can change your fate. Skillful aim and timing keep you alive, but luck determines what kind of power you’ll get. Power-ups spawn randomly like in Mario Kart, giving each fight an unpredictable twist. Will you get a blessing or a curse? In this world of wild risk and reward, every second counts and every shot could turn the tide.

# Game Synopsis

Lucky Son of a Gun drops players into a pixel wild-west world ruled by chaos and chance. Every gunfight is a test of both reflex and luck, with power-ups that completely alter the flow of battle. As players fight through waves of enemies, they collect random power-ups that can grant massive boosts or unpredictable side effects. The combination of skill, timing, and randomness keeps every run fresh and full of surprises. Every round feels unique, fast, and full of risky decisions.

Game Objective

The player’s goal is to survive shootouts and defeat all enemies in each level by balancing skillful combat with smart use of random power-ups. The game plays like a roguelike, where every run offers a new mix of power-ups and challenges. Success depends on quick reflexes, efficient use of whatever random boost you receive, and tactical movement to avoid enemy fire. Between rounds, players can unlock or upgrade different types of power-ups that appear in the world, allowing for progression and strategy. The ultimate goal is to survive as long as possible and set a new high score in the chaos of the wild west.

# Game Mechanics

- Movement: Use WASD or arrow keys to dodge bullets and maneuver strategically.

- Aiming & Shooting: Use the mouse to aim and fire at enemies in real time.

- Random Power-Up System: Defeated enemies and crates drop power-ups that provide temporary abilities or weapons — like rapid fire, explosive rounds, or shield boosts. Each pickup is randomized, keeping gameplay unpredictable and exciting.

- Roguelike Progression: After each round, players can unlock new power-up types or upgrade existing ones to change the odds in their next run.
