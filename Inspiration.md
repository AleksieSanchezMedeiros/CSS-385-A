Minimum one paragraph summary of inspiration you're drawing from to build your game

Something which has inspired me many times to try to make a game has been the tabletop roleplaying game Cyberpunk Red.
Particularly I want to try to copy the random dice roll aspect of the game as it always adds a sense of dread and the payoff feels huge when it goes your way.
The game also has almost like a skill list which would lower the amount of luck required and that's something I want to try to emulate too.
Apart from Cyberpunk Red, I'd like to borrow a mechanic from Red Dead, their bullet time mechanic that lets you aim at enemies that gets better as you progress.
Something which I always find cool would be physics either for the player and enemy ragdolls as it can probably add a level of complexity to the game. Something like rocket jumping would be cool.
